Detailed Description

mixed-mode AD: forward mode, with opportunistic expression-level reverse

Based on the technique for expression-level reverse mode in https://www.osti.gov/servlets/purl/1118331 but modified to fall back to forward-mode for small expressions and to use modern language features.
